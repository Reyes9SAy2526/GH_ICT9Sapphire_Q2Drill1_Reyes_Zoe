Be ready for any emergency. Here's what every Go Bag should contain.

First Aid Kit

Includes bandages, antiseptic wipes, medicines, and medical tools for basic emergencies.

Flashlight & Batteries

Provides light during blackouts. Always keep extra batteries!

Water Bottle

Store clean drinking water to stay hydrated when resources are limited.

Non-Perishable Food

Canned goods, protein bars, or biscuits to keep you nourished in any situation.

Whistle

Helps signal for help and alert others during emergencies or rescues.

Important Documents

Keep IDs, insurance papers, and emergency contacts sealed in a waterproof folder.
